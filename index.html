About

Welcome to the about section. Here you can learn more about this application and its features.

This application is designed to provide a seamless user experience, including a feed, chat functionality, profile management, and customizable settings.

Settings

Profile Settings
  • Avatar: Change or update your profile picture.
  • Username: Set or update your username.
  • Bio: Edit a short bio or description.
  • Status: Update your status (e.g., "Available," "Do Not Disturb").
  • Last Seen & Online Status: Set visibility of your online status.
  • Privacy Level:
    • Public Feeder: Allow sharing of messages/comments anonymously in feeds.
    • Private Feeder: Share content anonymously from private chats without displaying personal info.
Chat and Feed Settings
  • Theme: Customize chat appearance (e.g., Light, Dark, Custom).
  • Language: Set your preferred language.
  • Global and Private Chat Preferences:
    • Like, comment, and forward options for messages.
    • Privacy control for forwarded messages to display or hide identity.
Gambling & Poll Features
  • Poll Preferences:
    • Allow participation in global polls.
    • Share gambling results in feed anonymously or with user consent.
  • Game Participation Settings: Choose to display or hide your gaming activities in the feed.
Gameplay Settings
  • Difficulty Level: Customize the difficulty for in-app games.
  • Achievements & Badges: Display or hide achievements in profile.
  • Coins and Level:
    • View current coin balance and level.
    • Control whether your level is visible to others.
Account and Data
  • Link Accounts: Link third-party accounts for login.
  • Backup Chats: Enable/disable chat and feed data backup.
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete account and data.
Friends, Groups, and Feeds
  • Friends & Groups Management:
    • View and manage friends and groups.
    • Control who can add you to groups.
  • Feed Visibility: Allow posts/comments in feeds based on privacy (public, friends only, anonymous).
  • Anonymous Forwarding: Choose to forward messages to feeds anonymously.
Audio and Graphics (for Game Features)
  • Sound Effects: Enable or disable chat and game sounds.
  • Graphics Quality: Adjust graphics settings (Low, Medium, High) for in-game visuals.
Advanced Settings
  • Developer Options (for beta features).
  • Performance Metrics: Enable performance tracking (e.g., FPS, server ping).
Help and Support
  • FAQ & Tutorials: Access frequently asked questions and game tutorials.
  • Contact Support: Reach support via chat or email.
  • Community Forum: Engage with the community for tips and discussions.
